History of M Electric

M Electric was founded in 2004 in the city of Dallas and has built a solid reputation based on its good work results, as well as the performance of its employees, and the collaboration of suppliers, contractors and above all in maintaining a solid relationship with its clients, which has allowed us to expand our services in the residential, commercial and industrial areas.


Mission

Our mission as a company is to meet the needs of our clients in a timely manner by providing them with a quality service.



View

Carry out our work respecting the codes and standards established by the NEC (National Electrical Code) and the regulations of each city in which the project is developed and thus provide our clients with jobs in a legal and safe manner.



Values

Social Values: Respect the codes and rules established by national and state entities.

Customer service values: Respect, Trust and Communication.

Our one and only priority is the customer satisfaction
